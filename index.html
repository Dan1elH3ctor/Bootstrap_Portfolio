    About
    I am a self-taught artist. my interest in art started at the age of 6
    when I first laid eyes on my brother's artworks. He is "naturally talented",
    whereas I started out tracing before I dived into freehand drawing. Through the years
    my skills gradually improved with accuracy and realism. I have dabbled in many mediums
    i.e. pencil drawing, painting (acrylic, oil & gouache paints), clay sculpting, wood carving
    and digital drawing/painting.
    My interest in coding started with my love for gaming. Seeing how gaming graphics and capabilities
    have improved drew me towards learning how the final product is put together.
    It was then that i realised if i wanted to play any part in game design i would need coding skills.
